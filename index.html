Blasteroid.io is an upcoming Multiplayer Online Space Shooter.
Avoid asteroids, shoot opponents, watch out for aliens!

Blasteroid follows the four golden pillars of modern .io games. These 4 pillars are:

1. Free to play - No signup required. Simply navigate to the URL and click play to begin!

2. PvP - Beating robots just isn’t as satisfying, is it?

3. Never ending gameplay - Play until you die, achieve as high a score as possible!

4. Survival perks - The longer you play, the more of an advantage you have against other players. One should not invest 15 minutes into a game for no reason, after all!

What’s Special About Blasteroid.io?

Most IO games today are a spin-off of Agar.io, the original mechanics that experienced profound success. Blasteroid.io is a little different. This game is not based primarily off of collision with other players. Instead, players shoot each other to eliminate opponents. Additionally, Blasteroid.io has alternative components that will eliminate players as well. To succeed, proactive gameplay is required.
